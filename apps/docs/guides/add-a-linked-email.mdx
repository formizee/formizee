### Prerequisites
To get the most out of this guide, you'll need to:

* [Create a Formizee account](https://dashboard.formizee.com)

## 1. Go to Settings
Now that we are inside the dashboard, we will click on the **"Settings"** button on the sidebar.
Then go to **Account > Linked Emails**

<Frame>
  <img className="block dark:hidden" src="../assets/guides/add-a-linked-email/1-light.png" alt="Settings Page" />
  <img className="hidden dark:block" src="../assets/guides/add-a-linked-email/1-dark.png"  alt="Settings Page"/>
</Frame>

## 2. Add a new email
Now press on the **"Add Email"** button and put the email that you want link with Formizee.

<Frame>
  <img className="block dark:hidden" src="../assets/guides/add-a-linked-email/2-light.png" alt="Add your email" />
  <img className="hidden dark:block" src="../assets/guides/add-a-linked-email/2-dark.png"  alt="Add your email"/>
</Frame>

This will send a email verification to the selected address.

## 3. Verify your email
After you verify your address through the mail sended by Formizee, you should see that your new address is `verified`

<Frame>
  <img className="block dark:hidden" src="../assets/guides/add-a-linked-email/3-light.png" alt="Verification finished" />
  <img className="hidden dark:block" src="../assets/guides/add-a-linked-email/3-dark.png"  alt="Verification finished"/>
</Frame>

## 5. Use your new linked email
Now you can go to a form and add your new address to the `target emails`, this will trigger a notification when a submission is upload.
